# Leave Me Alone Privacy Policy

## Summary

Leave Me Alone is designed to hide selected Facebook and YouTube feed surfaces. It does not require an account and does not intentionally collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Data the extension uses

The extension stores your content visibility preferences locally using the browser storage API. This includes whether you want Facebook Reels, Facebook video surfaces, Facebook Stories, and YouTube Shorts hidden.

## Data sharing

Leave Me Alone does not send your settings, browsing history, page contents, credentials, or personal information to any remote server controlled by the developer.

## Permissions

The extension requests access to Facebook and YouTube pages only so it can identify and hide the supported feed surfaces on those sites. It requests local storage only to remember your toggle settings.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the public policy page and Chrome Web Store disclosures should be updated together before a new version is submitted.
